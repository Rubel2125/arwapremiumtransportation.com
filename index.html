We understand that medical transportation is more than just a ride — it's peace of mind for patients and families. Arwa Premium Transportation is proud to offer Non-Emergency Medical Transportation (NEMT) across Cumberland County and South Jersey.

We work with NJ Medicaid managed care organizations and NEMT brokers including Modivcare to provide covered transportation for eligible patients. Our drivers are trained in passenger assistance and are committed to making every medical journey comfortable and stress-free.
